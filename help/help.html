Quick Tips

  • Tap the configure button on the Home screen to choose the types of records to show in the "Go To" section and the order in which they are listed.
  • This icon indicates that you can expand and collapse panels to show/hide additional details.
  • Underlined text in detail views signifies a link action (such as browsing to a URL or jumping to an Account).
  • Tap My Activities to see all of the activities for which you are a leader or a member.
  • The "Clear Storage" option under Settings is strictly a trouble-shooting device to clear locally stored configuration.

Quick Actions

  • Following phone call or email actions, a view enabling you to type notes and save to history displays.
  • The "View address" action takes you to the native map application (on supported devices).
  • Quick Actions offer quick access to frequently-used actions (make a call, add a note, schedule an activity). Options vary depending on the record type.

Search

  • Key a term into search to locate information stored in the Tickets, Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities, Activities, Notes/History, and Leads records types.
